Tasting Guide

Follow these steps to fully experience the difference that Milbok Double Milk makes.

01

First Taste & Dirty Coffee

Start by tasting Milbok on its own and compare it directly with regular milk. Notice the richer texture and natural sweetness. Then make your Dirty Coffee ‚Äî pour the espresso very slowly over the back of a spoon so it floats on top of the cold Milbok. If it sinks, try pouring directly from the portafilter in a slow, thin stream.

What to notice

  • Natural sweetness and thicker consistency compared to regular milk
  • Clean layering ‚Äî dark espresso floating on white milk
  • Temperature contrast: hot espresso meeting ice-cold Milbok
02

Flat Double White

Make two Flat Whites ‚Äî one with regular milk, one with Milbok Double Milk. Steam both to the same microfoam consistency and pull identical espresso shots. Now taste them side by side. Focus on the difference in acidity, sweetness and texture with every sip.

What to notice

  • Noticeably lower acidity in the Milbok version
  • Enhanced natural sweetness ‚Äî no sugar needed
  • Richer, creamier mouthfeel and more stable microfoam
03

Matcha Double White

Prepare your matcha paste with hot water as usual, then steam Milbok Double Milk instead of regular milk. The extra density softens matcha's natural bitterness while amplifying its earthy, umami notes ‚Äî creating a smooth, vibrantly green drink with a velvety texture that is hard to forget.

What to notice

  • Reduced bitterness compared to matcha with regular milk
  • Deeper, earthier flavour with natural sweetness coming through
  • Silky smooth texture and more vibrant green colour
04

Hot Chocolate
